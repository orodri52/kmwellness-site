Medical nutrition therapy (MNT) is a clinical, evidence-based approach where a
registered dietitian uses your health history, labs, and goals to build a
nutrition plan that treats — not just manages — the root of the problem. Here
are five signs it may be right for you.

## 1. You have a new or chronic diagnosis

Conditions like type 2 diabetes, prediabetes, high blood pressure, high
cholesterol, and PCOS all respond strongly to targeted nutrition changes. MNT
is often covered by insurance for these diagnoses.

## 2. Weight won't budge despite "doing everything right"

If you've cut calories and added exercise with little to show for it, the issue
is often metabolic rather than a lack of willpower. A [metabolic assessment](/metabolic-assessment-el-paso/)
can reveal what your body is actually doing with the food you eat.

## 3. Your energy crashes every afternoon

Blood-sugar swings are one of the most common — and most fixable — causes of the
mid-afternoon slump. Small changes to meal timing and composition often help
within days.

## 4. You're navigating digestive issues

Bloating, reflux, and irregularity frequently trace back to diet. A dietitian
can help you identify triggers without unnecessary, restrictive elimination
diets.

## 5. You want results that actually last

Crash diets fail because they're not sustainable. MNT focuses on habits that fit
your real life in El Paso — your schedule, your budget, and your culture.
