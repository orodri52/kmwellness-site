A metabolic assessment takes the guesswork out of weight loss by measuring how
your body actually burns energy. Here's what your first visit at KM Wellness
Center looks like.

## Before you arrive

We'll ask you to avoid eating, exercising, and caffeine for a few hours
beforehand so your readings reflect your resting state. We'll confirm the exact
prep when you book.

## What we measure

- **Resting metabolic rate (RMR):** the calories your body burns at rest — the
  single most useful number for setting an accurate calorie target.
- **Body composition:** the ratio of muscle to fat, which matters far more than
  the number on a scale.
- **Health history and labs:** so your plan accounts for any medical conditions.

## How long it takes

Plan for about 45–60 minutes. The breathing measurement itself is quiet and
comfortable — you simply relax while we collect the data.

## What happens with your results

Your dietitian translates the numbers into a concrete plan: a realistic calorie
and protein target, a meal structure that fits your routine, and a follow-up
schedule. If appropriate, we'll discuss [medically supervised weight-loss options](/services/weight-loss-prescription-disease-management/).

## Why it beats generic calorie calculators

Online calculators estimate your metabolism from averages. A measured RMR can
differ from those estimates by hundreds of calories — which is often the
difference between progress and frustration.
